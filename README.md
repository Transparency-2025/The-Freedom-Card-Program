## üåü Program Overview & Mission Statement

The Beara Drive Initiative is a partnership between Scoil Phobail Bh√©ara and local industry.

> **Mission:** To empower the youth of the Beara Peninsula with essential mobility skills while reinvesting in the local economy. We aim to remove the financial barrier to driving for students of Scoil Phobail Bh√©ara, ensuring they are road-ready for employment and education by age 19.
>
> **The Mechanism:** Funds are distributed via the **"Beara Advantage Card"** (a restricted gift card). This creates a circular economy where education funding supports local business, as the card is redeemable *only* at participating local retailers, cafes, and service providers in Castletownbere and West Cork.

## ‚úÖ Core Strategy: The Hybrid Milestone Model

To ensure students don't just take the money and quit, but also have support upfront, a tiered approach is used.

### üí∞ Tier 1: The Starter Boost (20% of funds)
Awarded upon provisional license acquisition and proof of booking the first driving lesson.

### üöÄ Tier 2: The Momentum Grant (40% of funds)
Awarded after completing EDT Lesson 6 (the halfway point).

### üèÅ Tier 3: The Completion Reward (40% of funds)
Awarded upon sitting the official driving test (regardless of pass/fail, rewarding the effort and commitment).

## ‚öôÔ∏è Technical Logistics & Implementation

This section details the critical steps for the gift card platform selection and the program roll-out timeline.

### üí≥ Technical Solution Recommendation
The most suitable mechanism to ensure funds are restricted to the local economy while maintaining retailer ease-of-use is the **Town & City Gift Card Platform (Option 1)** or a **Restricted Loop Visa**.

* **Benefit:** The card runs on the Mastercard/Visa network but is "locked" to a specific list of local businesses (via Merchant Category Code or geographic restriction).
* **Retailer Impact:** Merchants do not need new hardware; they process the card like any standard transaction.

| Timeline | Action Item |
| :--- | :--- |

| **Month 3** | **Soft Launch:** Select 5 Senior Cycle students to pilot the program. |
| **Month 6** | **Expansion:** Open to all eligible students; expand retailer radius to Glengarriff/Bantry. |
