## Section I: Program Foundation & Strategy

**Program Mission:** To empower the youth of the Beara Peninsula with essential mobility skills while reinvesting in the local economy.

### Program Names & Structure (Ref: Strategy Document)

* **Primary Name:** The Beara Drive Initiative (or Green Light Beara)
* **Card Name:** The Beara Advantage Card
* **The Hybrid Milestone Model:** Funding is released in three key tiers to incentivize completion:
    * **Tier 1 (Starter Boost):** Upon Provisional License and Lesson 1 booking.
    * **Tier 2 (Momentum Grant):** Upon completing EDT Lesson 6.
    * **Tier 3 (Completion Reward):** Upon sitting the final RSA Driving Test.
